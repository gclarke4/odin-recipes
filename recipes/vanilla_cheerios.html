Homemade Vanilla Cheerios

    Ingredients
  • 50 grams Plain Cheerios
  • 20 grams Vanilla Whey Protein Powder (Optimum Nutrition)
  • 2 cups Milk (Whole recommended for the best flavor)

    Steps
  1. Pour Cheerios into a bowl.
  2. Pour milk into a glass.
  3. Add 5/6 grams of the protein powder at a time to the milk and mix thoroughly.
  4. Repeat until all protein powder is used.
  5. Enjoy!
