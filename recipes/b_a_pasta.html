Broccoli and Cheddar Pasta

    Ingredients
  • 80 grams Broccoli Florets
  • 2 1/2 oz Protein Penne from Barilla
  • 28 grams Shredded Cheese
  • 14 grams Smart Balance Butter
  • 1 tsp Nature's Seasoning OR Greek Seasoning (add more if desired)
    Steps
  1. Fill a pot with adequate amount of water and bring water to a boil.
  2. Once boiling, add the penne and let it cook for 8-10 minutes.
  3. While penne is cooking, pan fry broccoli in the butter and cover it.
  4. Add penne and broccoli to bowl and add shredded cheese and mix.
  5. Finish with the seasoning chosen earlier.
  6. Enjoy!
