Savory Oats

NOTE: You need an instant-pot for this recipe!

    Ingredients
  • 1 1/3 cups Steel Cut Oats
  • 4 cups Stock or Bone Broth (Low Sodium is preferred)
  • 1/2 tsp Italian Seasoning
  • 1/2 tsp Garlic Powder
  • 1 tsp Umami powder
  • 1/2 tsp Smoked Paprika
  • 1/2 tsp Salt

    Steps
  1. Add all ingredients to the instant-pot.
  2. Cook for 4 minutes on high.
  3. Natural release the instant-pot for 15 minutes.
  4. Optional: Top with an egg for great flavor.
  5. Enjoy!
